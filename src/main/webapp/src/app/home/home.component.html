Overview

This website provides documentation for the completely free Anki decks that allow you to learn music-theory, scales, chords, rhythms, and more. The decks are available for download on the AnkiWeb website, no registration, no email, no cookies!

Learn more in less time

You want to get better at playing your instrument? Then you have to practice! To practice means to learn by repetition. But when and how often should you repeat something to memorize quickly and long lasting? There are tons of recommendations for practice schedules on Youtube, but Anki is different. Anki implements a so called spaced repetition learning method, which has been shown by research to be superior to other learning methods.

About Anki

Anki is a free and open source learning software platform, that allows you to learn almost anything using spaced repetition. Anki content is organized in cards and decks of cards. The front side of a card contains a question, and the back side contains the answer. First, you are presented with the question, then you think of the answer, next you compare your answer with the back side of the card, and finally you rate yourself: was my answer correct? Did I remember quickly or did it take too much time? Depending on your rating, you will be challenged with this card more or less often in the future.

There is much more about Anki. I will not try to explain everything here, there is the Anki Manual and hundreds of videos on Youtube doing a much better job.

About AnkiJazz

AnkiJazz (this site) provides musical content to Anki. For example, the front side of a card may ask you to play the F-Major scale. Then you play that scale on your instrument. Next you open the back-side of the card which shows notation and plays audio of the correct F-Major scale. Finally you rate yourself - was it correct? Did I play quickly or did it take too much time? Depending on your rating, you will be challenged to play F-Major Scale more or less often in the future.
